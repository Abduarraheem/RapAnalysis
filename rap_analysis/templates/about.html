About


This project is centralized around our algorithm for what is called a rhyme scheme analysis.

A rhyme scheme is defined as the pattern according to which end rhymes (rhymes located at the end of lines) are repeated in works poetry. With this webapp we are applying this analysis to rap music.

We hope to make this more interesting to the user by making it possible for them to link this with their spotify account so they can access the songs in which they listen to regularly and more accessible by presenting them with a version in which they dont have to access a spotify account as not all people will have one.
